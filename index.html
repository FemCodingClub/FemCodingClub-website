Bootcamp Information

Technology is everywhere: social media, music apps, games or films and series. What is your favourite part of it?

Join our Girls Only Coding Bootcamp and you will get a hands-on interaction with the tech world!

What?

A free 6 weeks girls only coding bootcamp, where you will build your own website using HTML, CSS and Javascript. We will provide all the needed resources including laptops and your teachers will be professionals from the tech world. And best part is that at the end of the bootcamp you'll get a really cool certificate!

Where?

In a really cool location in the heart of Barcelona, the Agbar Tower.

When?

Starting March 7th until April 11th, every Tuesday from 18:00 to 20:00. 6 weeks in total.

Who?

Girls aged between 15 and 18. There's a limited number of students since you'll have to work in groups, so join right away!
